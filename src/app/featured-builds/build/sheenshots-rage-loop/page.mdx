> From the video description:
>
> Recently I decided to try this SKILL LOOP build you can pull off with two of the strongest abilities in Remnant 2. And It's about as good as it sounds. You gain access to extremely high-ranged damage for an infinite amount of time. Depending on which automatic gun you decide to use you're high crit chance grants you back those skills over and over. I decided to mess around will a bunch of guns for this run and see which were good. All of them work decently well, but there are three specific options that you want for optimization. Plasma Cutter, Monarch, and the Chicago Typewriter.
>
